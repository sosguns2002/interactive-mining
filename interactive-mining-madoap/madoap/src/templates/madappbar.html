Terms explanation

    View Count (VC):
    Number of times that a publication's metadata has been browsed.
    Download Counts (DC):
    Number of times that a publication has been downloaded.
    Hit index (HI):
    Sum of VC and DC.
    Popularity Index (PI):
    Weighted combination of VC and DC, putting more emphasis on DC.
